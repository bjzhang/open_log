Job Description
We are looking for a Linux Kernel Developer for our Virtualization solutions in SUSE Linux Enterprise and openSUSE.
 
In this role you will work with our global team of virtualization experts on a broad range of kernel topics related to virtualization in general. A solid understanding of the Linux kernel and the x86-64 hardware architecture are essential for this position. Being familiar with virtualization concepts on the Intel/AMD platform is helpful, but not a must.
 
 
Other expected skills:
 
Experience with low-level Linux Kernel development
Experience with developing in and contributing to open source projects
Very good English communication skills
