Linux Kernel Storage Developer

Tracking Code

Job Description
    Linux Kernel Developer develops and maintains Linux Kernel subsystems both in SUSE Linux software products and in the open source upstream kernel with a particular focus to storage technologies.
    He represents himself actively and shares his knowledge and further develops it in special interest groups, professional organizations and specialized conferences.

Key responsibilities:
        design and development of storage-related Linux Kernel subsystems (SCSI, SAS, FC, FCoE, iSCSI, Multipath, DM, MD, ...)
        technical analysis of problems and errors in the Linux Kernel
        performance analysis and tuning
        activities connected with participation in professional organizations and specialized conferences

Knowledge required:
        excellent knowledge of the C programming language
        excellent knowledgeof the storage-related subsystems of the Linux Kernel
        good knowledge of open-source community principles, with main focus on Linux Kernel development model and practices
        good knowledge of contemporary server and storage hardware and interconnects
        good knowledge of assembler language
        good knowledge of Linux debugging tools
        good spoken and written knowledge of English

Characteristics required:
        accuracy and thoroughness
        consideration and assertiveness
        independence
        flexibility and ability to work under pressure

We offer:
        the opportunity to participate directly in the creation of the Linux operating systems
        the possibility to use 10% of your working time for professional development
        direct cooperation with members of numerous free/open-source software projects from all over the world
        flexible weekly working time
